Problem

Microplastics are now detected in human blood, organs, and placental tissue.

Opportunity

Approach

Why Now

Microplastics have crossed a threshold—from environmental contaminant to human health variable. What was previously unmeasurable is now quantifiable, and what was theoretical is now observable.

▸ First peer-reviewed evidence of biological accumulation
▸ Escalating regulatory and public health attention
